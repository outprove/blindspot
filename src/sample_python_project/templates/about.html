About Blindspot

Blindspot is a place for thoughtful questions, outside perspective, and honest reflection.


Despite our best efforts and self-reflection, there are things that we cannot (or perhaps will not) see. It takes an outside perspective. But those who love us are rarely that direct with us in order to save our feelings.


This tool allows us to ask the questions we want answered and a safe place for people to answer them.

Answers are totally anonymous!

We have done our best to make this experience totally anonymous for the person answering the question. We don't record the IP address, don't ask for a name or email address. Consequently there is a risk that someone could spam an answer if your page is disseminated widely enough. You can always change your profile ID in that case. And while we'd like to prevent all cases of spam and abuse, a truly anonymous system will have that risk.
