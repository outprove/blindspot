What is your Blindspot?

This site helps people surface the questions they may be too close to answer on their own. Each user can publish one focused question, invite anonymous responses, and review outside perspectives in one place.

How do I use this site?

  1. Create an account by registering and validating your email. No other personal information is required.
  2. Customize your profile id which is the only thing to publicly identify you.
  3. Think of a question you want to ask.
  4. Share your question and personal URL to whomever you like.
Purpose

Ask better questions

Use a single clear question to gather perspectives you might otherwise miss.

Responses

Collect anonymous insight

Return to your dashboard
